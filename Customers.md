## Customers

A Customer is a shopper who has created an account on the storefront. Customer Accounts allow shoppers to login and manage the following:

- Order Messages
- Viewing Orders
- Reordering Products
- Return Requests
- Address Book
- Stored Payment Methods
- Wishlists
- Account Settings
- Recently Viewed Products
- Store Credit
